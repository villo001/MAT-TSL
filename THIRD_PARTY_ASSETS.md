# Third-Party Assets

This document lists all third-party assets used in the project  
**MAT: The Sunless Lands (MAT:TSL)** and their respective licenses.

All assets are used in compliance with their licenses.

---

## Scope

This file covers:
- Fonts
- Icons
- Audio (music and sound effects)
- Visual assets (sprites, UI, tilesets)

Assets located under the `tests/` directory are used **for prototyping and experimentation only**  
and are **not included in the final game build** unless explicitly stated.
